Engineering tools for education

Students not understanding lectures for all academic levels. From what I've seen from my years of attending grade school to first-year university, it is easy to observe that the overall learning in the classroom is very inefficient than as to what it could be. The lectures given by the person talking in front whether they be a teacher or a student, are always either structured in a way that perceives the audience having knowledge so much more, or so much less than what they actually have regarding that subject.

However solving this problem would take a daunting amount of time, the more viable solution left-behind would be to just go around it. Such as giving a short-summary just enough for the audience to piece together the current topic by themselves. This in exchange gives a different problem, as how can this now be done efficiently. This is where software for education comes in.

With the use of graphical frameworks, interfaces, whether MICROSOFT_WORD, MANIM, POWERPOINT, or BLENDER having a tool to present the idea across while abstracting the idea itself into something is easier for the majority to understand. The drawback to this approach would be the initial setup and learning curve in using whatever and many tools are chosen.

Crypto the new norm

Many people nowadays prefer the use of digital currency instead of bringing actual physical cash. The ability to access your deposit from any place with internet connection is more convenient than going to the nearest bank or ATM to withdraw some cash.

Digital transactions are common occurrences in this day and age, especially with the existence of shopping apps. And due to its ease of use and the spike of digital adaption during the COVID-19 pandemic, many physical businesses today give their customers the option to pay using digital currency. Digitalized transaction allows the ability to have purchases globally from anywhere in the world, and also have the ability to pay on- time or a head of time.

There are also other benefits in most digital currency such as crypto using BLOCKCHAIN, which is used to decentralize transactions. Meaning transactions are not reliant on a central figure like the bank, but is maintained by a network of workers. Most crypto currency however are not tied to a stable exchange rate, a good example of this would be BITCOIN wherein the value fluctuates each day.

MY AI OVERLORDS

Machine learning, AI, the latest trend. The fast pace development of computer technologies leave everyone worried and amazed. Worried that they would not be able to keep-up, that the speed in which they are learning about the newest technologies are behind that of the speed in which they are developed.

The average person has turned to AI as a means of understanding knowledge, and summarizing text in less than a quarter of the time it would take them to do it themselves. Whilst researchers create their own programs, using them for discovering new information via rapid trial and error which is possible by the current capabilities of computer hardware.

AI has had many contributions in modern research or in text generation for the average person, and thus the demand for AI continues to increase. However the more the AI model scales the more computational power it would consume, the model would now be limited by the capabilities of their hardware. Thus companies invest not in only creating the newest AI model, but also in the creation of the best computer hardware and server hardware.
